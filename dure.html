Dure Negasa

Dure has served the displaced/destitute Ethiopian Oromo Christians living in Kenya for 24 years. As a visionary of the North Kenya mission outreach, operated by the Oromo Christian Fellowship in Nairobi (OCFN), her focus is harvesting souls and planting churches.
